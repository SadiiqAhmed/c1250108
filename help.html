Help & How-To Guide

Using the navigation bar

The main navigation is fixed at the top of every page. Use the links to jump between Home, Departments (Gallery), Book Appointment, Help, and About. The current page is highlighted.

Browsing departments & doctors

Visit the Departments page (Gallery). You'll see department images and a detailed doctor table. Hover over rows for highlights; zebra stripes make reading easier. External medical links open in a new tab.

Filling out the appointment form

On the Book Appointment page, complete all required fields (marked with *). Select your department, preferred date, and add any symptoms. Submit — a confirmation will appear. All fields use HTML5 validation.

Finding location & contact

Visit the About page for our address, a Google Map, phone, email, and opening hours. The footer also contains quick contact info on every page.
